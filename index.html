Pick Your Power

Trinity College



Using John Ratey’s book, “Spark” as a cornerstone, Pick Your Power™ is a program of activity that helps you “beat stress, sharpen your thinking, lift your mood, and improve your memory.”





  • 6 Week Program begins with a meeting with a Dietician.
  • You will receive a nutritional plan, Fitbit, phone apps, a schedule of 1-hour long group exercise classes on campus, and a list of resources on campus to support your healthy lifestyle
  • Utilize a combination of rigorous exercise and mindful movements to ensure a total package of well-being between your mind and body
  • Check your progress on your apps and Fitbit at the end of the 6 weeks and see how you did!!!
