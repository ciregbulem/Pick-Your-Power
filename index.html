Fitness Programs

Trinity College Recreation is dedicated to providing the community with enriching opportunities that improve the mental, emotional and physical well-being of our members. Check out some of programs:

Fitness Pass


The Fitness Pass gives students access to weekly fitness sessions including Cycling, Yoga, Weight-Training and even Zumba! Classes are all taught by our fabulous instructors 5 days a week! Sign for your pass today to get started on a journey to a healthier YOU!

Pick Your Power


  • 6 Week Program begins with a meeting with a Dietician.
  • You will receive a nutritional plan, Fitbit, phone apps, a schedule of 1-hour long group exercise classes on campus, and a list of resources on campus to support your healthy lifestyle
  • Utilize a combination of rigorous exercise and mindful movements to ensure a total package of well-being between your mind and body
  • Check your progress on your apps and Fitbit at the end of the 6 weeks and see how you did!!!
  • You will have the option to purchase the Fitbit at the end of the 6 weeks or turn it in
